Details

Attire

Please wear whatever you feel comfortable in. We’ll be outside, so dress for the weather.

Location

Devils Elbow 2 🤘 is located on Hemlock Dr in North Park, McCandless Twp, Allison Park, PA.

Hemlock Dr is right off Babcock Blvd, and close to the Black and Gold Playground and North Park Swimming Pool.

Google Maps can give you more specific directions.

Parking

The space has its own dedicated access road and parking lot. There’s also a large parking lot across the street at the North Park Swimming Pool.

Food & Drinks

We’ll have pizza, snacks, and sweets, which we think will pair nicely with beer, soda and water. Think wedding reception meets picnic meets pizza party.

Entertainment

Expect a playlist with plenty of hit songs from the 80s and 90s, and some outdoor games.

Accomodations

Unfortunately, you cannot sleep in the park. If you need somewhere to stay nearby, the Fairfield Inn & Suites at McCandless Crossing appears to be a fine choice. Please know that we have not reserved any rooms.

Backup plan

This is our backup plan; rain or shine.

COVID-19 and safety

We acknowledge that there’s still an ongoing global pandemic. Despite this, we feel OK proceding with our celebratation.

We’re taking the following precautions:
Sanitizing everything beforehand; hand sanitizer on tap
Providing ample space between picnic tables
Limiting the number of guests to 30

We understand if you decide not to attend due to COVID-19. We’ll look forward to celebrating with you in the future.
